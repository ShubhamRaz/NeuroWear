Sensor Details

Electrodermal Activity (EDA) Sensors

To monitor stress levels and emotional responses by measuring skin conductance.(Empatica E4)

Photoplethysmo graphy (PPG) Sensors

For tracking heart rate and blood oxygen levels.(Maxim Integrated MAX30102)

Electromyography (EMG) Sensors

To detect muscle activity and body movement.(Myoware Muscle Sensor)

Temperature Sensors

To monitor body temperature and adjust the fabric for comfort.(Texas Instruments TMP117)

Electroencephalo graphy (EEG) Sensors

For analyzing brainwave patterns to detect emotional states.(OpenBCI Ultracortex)

Respiration Sensors

To measure breathing patterns for relaxation monitoring.(Vernier Respiration Belt)

Inertial Measurement Units (IMU)

Including accelerometers, gyroscopes, and magnetometers for tracking body motion and posture.(Bosch Sensortec BMI160)

Haptic Feedback Actuators

For providing gentle vibrations or other sensory feedback based on emotional states.(Precision Microdrives 310-103)

Pressure Sensors

To detect body posture and distribute pressure for ergonomic comfort.(Honeywell FSS Series)

Gas Sensors

To monitor environmental air quality and adjust scent diffusion.(MQ-135 Air Quality Sensor)

Key Features

Built-In Biometric Sensors

Heart rate, skin temperature, and sweat level sensors monitor your stress in real time.

Neuro-Responsive Fabric

Micro-fabric layers adjust temperature and pressure to match your mood and energy levels.

Scent Diffusion

Releases calming or energizing scents to boost well-being without intrusive devices.

AI-Powered Adjustments

Proprietary AI analyzes data patterns to deliver personalized comfort instantly.

How to Use

Step 1: Wear the NeuroWear Jacket like any normal jacket.

Step 2: Turn on the built-in AI module via the discreet button on the cuff.

Step 3: Sensors automatically monitor your stress, fatigue, and mood levels.

Step 4: The fabric adjusts temperature/pressure while scent diffusers activate based on AI analysis.

This ensures a personalized, non-intrusive way to manage your well-being—no medication or bulky devices needed!
