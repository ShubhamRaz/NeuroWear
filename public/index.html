World’s First AI-Powered Clothing

Many struggle with stress, anxiety, or low energy throughout the day. NeuroWear’s innovative jacket detects stress, fatigue, or low mood and adjusts temperature, pressure, and scent diffusion to improve your well-being instantly.

The Problem & Our Solution

Problem: There’s no real-time, non-intrusive solution to regulate mood without medication or bulky devices.

Solution: NeuroWear’s built-in biometric sensors and neuro-responsive fabric adapt to your mood on the fly, offering immediate comfort and relief.

Sensor Details

Heart Rate Sensor

Uses optical technology to continuously monitor your pulse for real-time heart rate tracking.

Temperature Sensor

Measures skin temperature with high precision for optimal thermal comfort.

Pressure Sensor

Detects subtle pressure variations to ensure the garment adapts to your movements.

Scent Diffusion Sensor

Monitors cues to control the release of calming or energizing aromas.

Accelerometer

Tracks movement and activity levels to adjust responses for your dynamic lifestyle.

Gyroscope

Measures orientation changes to ensure the jacket adapts seamlessly to your posture.

Humidity Sensor

Monitors moisture levels to manage sweat and maintain comfort.

Key Features

Built-In Biometric Sensors

Heart rate, skin temperature, and sweat level sensors monitor your stress in real time.

Neuro-Responsive Fabric

Micro-fabric layers adjust temperature and pressure to match your mood and energy levels.

Scent Diffusion

Releases calming or energizing scents to boost well-being without intrusive devices.

AI-Powered Adjustments

Proprietary AI analyzes data patterns to deliver personalized comfort instantly.

How to Use

Step 1: Wear the NeuroWear Jacket like any normal jacket.

Step 2: Turn on the built-in AI module via the discreet button on the cuff.

Step 3: Sensors automatically monitor your stress, fatigue, and mood levels.

Step 4: The fabric adjusts temperature/pressure while scent diffusers activate based on AI analysis.

This ensures a personalized, non-intrusive way to manage your well-being—no medication or bulky devices needed!
